My top 3 at all

Game of thrones

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO. It is an adaptation of A Song of Ice and Fire, George R. R. Martin's series of epic fantasy novels, the first of which is A Game of Thrones.

Dark

Dark is a German science fiction thriller streaming television series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. It follows an increasing number of disappearances that are affecting the small German town of Winden, which exposes the relationships among four estranged families as they uncover a time travel conspiracy that spans several generations.

Money heist

Money Heist is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. The series traces two long-prepared heists led by the Professor (Álvaro Morte), one on the Royal Mint of Spain, and one on the Bank of Spain.
